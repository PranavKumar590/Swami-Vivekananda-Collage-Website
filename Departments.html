Language

VISION

To constantly upgrade students' integrated language skills and mould character through various literary genres.

MISSION

Making language skills not only a tool for ordinary interaction but emphasizing on different components of it; so to develop linguistic competence and confidence in both written and oral discourses, while building the power of perception, reasoning, comprehending and logical making.

OBJECTIVE

    Enable our students to read, write and think well.

    Decode, analyze and interpret authentic texts of different genres.

    Produce organized, coherent discourse in oral and written modes.

Science

VISION

Science department is committed to helping students becoming critical consumers of scientific information related to their everyday lives.

MISSION

The mission of the Science department is for all students to have some appreciation of the beauty and wonder of science; possess sufficient knowledge of science; engage in public discussions on related issues; are able to continue to learn about science throughout their lives; and have skills of a scientifically literate person in their respective science discipline.

OBJECTIVE

    To create awareness in students about the world they live in, the key role of science and its effects on the society and to educate the students on environment sustainability.

    To ensure that students acknowledge and use the scientific method (problem, hypothesis, experiment, conclusion) in decision making.

    To develop in students the desirable scientific attitudes and values like collaboration, team spirit, fellow feeling, leadership, courage, truthfulness, honesty and sincerity.

Home Economics

VISION

Spiritually, prepare students for life's challenges.

MISSION

The department is committed to provide quality education in Home economics to ensure a harmonious development of learners in terms of academic excellence, acquisition of skills and universal values for the enrichment of each individual, the community and the nation.

OBJECTIVE

    To empower students to learn more about the home, the family and the society

    To use and manage human and material resources to benefit the changing circumstances in families, society and the environment

    To acquire the management theory and skills and apply the principles involved to all areas of their lives, both at present and in the future.
